We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use Echord.

---

<Heading id='data-we-collect' title='Data We Collect' />

- **Usage Data**: We collect information about how you interact with Echord, including API requests, response times, and error logs. This data helps us improve the service and troubleshoot issues.
- **Device Information**: We may collect information about the device you use to access Echord, including device type, operating system, and browser type.
- **Cookies and Tracking Technologies**: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on Echord. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.
- **Authentication Data (if applicable)**: If you sign in via third-party services (e.g., GitHub, Spotify), we only store essential metadata (e.g., user ID, access token) as needed for functionality and never share or sell this information.
  - All sensitive data is stored in a secure environment and is encrypted at rest and in transit.

<Heading id='how-we-use-your-data' title='How We Use Your Data' />

- **Service Improvement**: We use the data we collect to enhance the performance, reliability, and security of Echord.
- **Analytics**: We analyze usage patterns to understand how users interact with Echord and identify areas for improvement.
- **API Functionality**: We use your data to provide and maintain the functionality of Echord, including processing API requests and generating responses.
- **User Support**: We may use your data to respond to inquiries, provide support, and troubleshoot issues.
- **Behavior Analytics**: We may use your data to analyze user behavior and improve the overall user experience.

<Heading id='data-sharing-and-disclosure' title='Data Sharing and Disclosure' />

- **Third-Party Services**: Echord does not share your data with third-party services unless explicitly stated.
  - Echord does not utilize third-party analytics services or tracking tools.
- **Anonymized Data**: We may share aggregated and anonymized data with third parties for research, analysis, or marketing purposes. This data does not identify individual users and cannot be used to trace back to you.
- **Legal Compliance**: We may disclose your data if required by law or in response to valid legal requests, such as subpoenas or court orders.

<Heading id='data-retention' title='Data Retention' />

- **Data Retention Period**: We retain your data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law.
- **Data Deletion**: You can request the deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. We will delete your data in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.
- **Data Backup**: We may retain backup copies of your data for a limited period to ensure data recovery in case of accidental loss or deletion.
- **Data Portability**: You have the right to request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format. We will provide this data to you upon request.

You can request the deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. We will delete your data in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

> Reach out to us at [contact@echord.uk](mailto:contact@echord.uk) for any inquiries or requests related to your data.

<Heading id='security' title='Security' />- **Data Protection**: We implement
reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access,
loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet or
electronic storage is 100% secure. - **Data Breach Notification**: In the event
of a data breach, we will notify affected users as required by applicable laws
and regulations. - **User Responsibility**: You are responsible for maintaining
the confidentiality of your account credentials and for all activities that
occur under your account. If you suspect any unauthorized use of your account,
you must notify us immediately. - **Data Encryption**: We use encryption to
protect sensitive data during transmission and storage. This includes using
HTTPS for secure communication and encrypting sensitive information at rest. -
**Access Controls**: We implement access controls to limit access to your data
to authorized personnel only. This includes using role-based access controls and
regularly reviewing access permissions. - **Regular Security Audits**: We
conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments to identify and
address potential security risks. This includes penetration testing and code
reviews.

<Heading id='your-rights' title='Your Rights' />

- **Access to Your Data**: You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you. We will provide you with a copy of your data upon request.

If you're using Echord without signing in, we do not collect any personal data from you. However, you can still access and use the service without creating an account. We encourage you to explore Echord and its features without any obligation to sign in.

<Heading id='contact-us' title='Contact Us' />

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at [contact@echord.uk](mailto:contact@echord.uk).
